Moon Phases

When planning a trip on the ocean it is important to look at the phases of the moon.

Moon phases influence tides. If you are prone to sea sickness avoid travel during the new and full moon.

If you are planning a sea kayaking trip and are a beginner or are traveling with people who want adventure without fighting the toughest currents plan to kayak during the first or third quarter phases of the moon.
